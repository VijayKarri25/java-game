How To Play

THE STORY

You are an aspiring Java Full Stack Developer entering the Code Realm. Each realm represents a real-world skill that companies demand. Defeat monsters by answering questions correctly to level up your skills and become job-ready.

BATTLE MECHANICS

Each battle presents a question with 4 options. Correct = deal damage to monster, gain XP. Wrong = lose HP, monster attacks. Answer 5 questions per round. Defeat the monster to progress.

STREAKS & BONUSES

Consecutive correct answers build a streak. Streak of 3+ gives bonus XP. Streak of 5+ gives HP recovery. Keep the streak alive for maximum rewards!

REALM PROGRESSION

Complete 60% of a realm to unlock the next one. Realms go from basics (Java Foundations) to advanced (Microservices, DevOps). Each question includes a real production usage example — you're learning what companies actually use.

HP & LEVELING

You start with 100 HP. Wrong answers cost 15 HP. Leveling up restores 30 HP. If HP reaches 0, the battle ends but your XP is saved. You can retry any realm anytime.

FOR NON-IT PEOPLE

Every explanation uses simple analogies. "Variable" = a labeled box. "API" = a waiter between customer and kitchen. "Database" = an organized filing cabinet. You don't need any prior coding experience — just read the explanations after each answer!
